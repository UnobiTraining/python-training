## What This Lesson Covers

This beginner lesson focuses on practical Python math, including:

- integers and floats
- checking type with `type()`
- casting values with `int()` and `float()`
- arithmetic operators
- comparison operators
- order of operations (PEMDAS)
- built-in math functions such as `abs()`, `round()`, `min()`, and `max()`
- floating-point precision
- floor division and modulus
- practical examples using time math
- increment and decrement shortcuts
- quick even/odd checks with modulus

## Why This Lesson Matters

Math shows up constantly in Python, even in beginner projects.

Students need to be comfortable with:
- storing numbers
- understanding decimals
- performing calculations
- comparing values
- formatting readable output

This lesson keeps the scope tight and practical so beginners can focus on the ideas they will actually use right away.
